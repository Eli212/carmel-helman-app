Common Questions

I didn't receive my sign-in link.
Check your spam folder, and make sure you entered the same email address your trainer registered for you. If it still doesn't arrive, contact us by email.

How do I cancel or change a class registration?
Open the schedule, tap the class you're registered to, and choose unregister. If the class is locked for changes, contact your trainer.

How do I delete my account?
Open the app, go to Profile and tap "Delete Account". Your account is deactivated immediately and all data is permanently deleted within 30 days. You can also email us at the address above.
